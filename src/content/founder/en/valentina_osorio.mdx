# ABOUT ME

I am a software programming student with a strong preference for web design. In addition to my interest in business and graphic design, I consider myself a positive, responsible, and well-mannered individual in all social settings. I am passionate about learning new things and innovating, always staying updated with the latest advancements in technology and design. My goal is to combine my technical and creative skills to develop visually appealing and functional digital solutions.

# EDUCATION  

- Proficient in Microsoft Office applications, including Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, and Access.  
- Technical High School Diploma in Software Programming.  

# EXPERIENCE  

I have had the opportunity to work on several development projects, including:  

- **iEstudiar**: An educational software developed in Python, where I contributed to the design. This project allowed me to apply my knowledge of design and development, contributing to an effective and functional educational tool.  

- **Hosting**: A property rental management software currently in development. This project has given me the opportunity to work on implementing features that improve property and tenant management efficiency and organization.  

- **Other Projects**: I am currently involved in other projects that continue to challenge me and help me grow as a developer. Each project provides new experiences and the chance to expand my technical skills.
